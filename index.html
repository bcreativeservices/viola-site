About Viola

Viola Mbise, PMHNP-BC, is a board-certified Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner dedicated to providing compassionate, non-judgmental care.

Her approach is grounded in evidence-based practice, cultural sensitivity, and a deep respect for each person's lived experience.

Viola believes that mental health care should feel safe, collaborative, and empowering—never rushed or one-size-fits-all.

Full Biography

Viola Mbise, PMHNP-BC, is a Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner with advanced training in the assessment, diagnosis, and treatment of mental health conditions across the lifespan.

She emphasizes a collaborative, whole-person approach and integrates each patient's strengths and challenges into a tailored treatment plan.

Patients describe Viola as warm, attentive, and easy to talk to.
