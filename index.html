About Viola

Viola Mbise, PMHNP-BC, is a board-certified Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner dedicated to providing compassionate, non-judgmental care. She partners with patients to understand their stories, values, and goals, and works collaboratively to create treatment plans that feel both effective and sustainable.

Her approach is grounded in evidence-based practice, cultural sensitivity, and a deep respect for each person's lived experience.

Viola believes that mental health care should feel safe, collaborative, and empowering—never rushed or one-size-fits-all.

Full Biography

Viola Mbise, PMHNP-BC, is a Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner with advanced training in the assessment, diagnosis, and treatment of mental health conditions across the lifespan. She has experience working with individuals navigating anxiety, depression, mood disorders, ADHD, and stress-related concerns.

In her practice, Viola emphasizes a collaborative, whole-person approach. She takes time to understand each patient's unique background, strengths, and challenges, and integrates this understanding into a tailored treatment plan that may include medication management, lifestyle recommendations, and coordination with other members of the care team when appropriate.

Patients often describe Viola as warm, attentive, and easy to talk to. She strives to create a space where patients feel heard, respected, and actively involved in their care decisions.

Clinical Focus

  • Depression and mood disorders
