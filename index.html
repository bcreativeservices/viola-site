Our Mission & Vision

Core Clarity Mental Health Services is built on the belief that everyone deserves accessible, respectful, and empowering mental health support.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, personalized, and culturally responsive mental health care that promotes clarity, healing, empowerment, and long-term wellness.

Our Vision

To become a trusted mental health partner in the community by helping individuals and families build healthier lives through accessible, evidence-based, and holistic behavioral health services.

Meet Your Provider

At Core Clarity Mental Health Services, your care is guided by a provider who values collaboration, respect, and whole-person wellness. You are seen as more than a diagnosis—your story, strengths, and goals matter.

Through a combination of evidence-based treatment, thoughtful medication management when appropriate, and supportive therapeutic approaches, we work together to help you move toward greater clarity, stability, and emotional resilience.

What We Offer

We offer comprehensive psychiatric and therapy services designed to support you through every stage of your mental health journey.

Psychiatric Evaluations

Comprehensive mental health assessments designed to understand your unique needs and create individualized treatment plans.

Medication Management

Thoughtful and collaborative psychiatric medication management focused on safety, symptom improvement, and overall wellness.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one therapy sessions that support emotional healing, coping skills development, and personal growth.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Evidence-based therapy focused on identifying and changing unhealthy thought patterns and behaviors to improve emotional well-being.

Trauma-Informed Care

Compassionate treatment approaches that recognize the impact of trauma and support recovery in a safe, empowering environment.

Anxiety & Depression Treatment

Support for managing symptoms related to anxiety disorders, depression, mood disorders, stress, and emotional overwhelm.

Telehealth Services

Convenient and secure virtual appointments that allow clients to access quality mental health care from the comfort of home.

Our Approach

At Core Clarity Mental Health Services, we believe mental health care should be collaborative, personalized, and empowering.

We take time to listen, understand your concerns, and partner with you to develop realistic goals that support your emotional and psychological well-being.

Our care philosophy emphasizes:

  • Compassionate and nonjudgmental support
  • Evidence-based treatment approaches
  • Cultural sensitivity and inclusivity
  • Whole-person wellness
  • Patient education and empowerment
  • Collaborative treatment planning

Our goal is not simply symptom management — it is helping you develop the tools, confidence, and resilience needed to thrive in everyday life.

Conditions We Commonly Support

We work with a wide range of mental health concerns and life challenges, always with a focus on your strengths and goals.

Anxiety Disorders

Support for generalized anxiety, panic, excessive worry, and fear that interferes with daily life.

Depression

Care for persistent sadness, low motivation, emotional numbness, and related mood symptoms.

PTSD & Trauma

Support for those who have experienced trauma and are seeking safety, healing, and recovery.

ADHD

Help with difficulties related to focus, organization, impulsivity, and follow-through.

Mood Disorders

Support for mood instability, bipolar spectrum conditions, and related emotional challenges.

Stress & Burnout

Care for overwhelm related to work, caregiving, or chronic stressors.

Adjustment Disorders

Support for emotional or behavioral symptoms in response to major life changes.

Grief & Loss

Compassionate support while navigating the emotional impact of loss and transition.

Relationship Challenges

Help with communication, boundaries, and emotional patterns in relationships.

Life Transitions

Support through changes in family, career, identity, and roles.

Insurance & Payments

We want you to feel informed and supported when it comes to using your benefits.

Please also note: As we are currently out of network with some insurances, we strongly encourage you to check your out-of-network benefits to see if you can apply them to our services. If we do not accept your insurance and you decide to utilize our services via self-pay for out-of-network benefits, we can provide a superbill, which you can submit to your insurance for reimbursement of a certain percentage of what you paid (your insurance company determines percentage or amount reimbursed). Please get in touch with your insurance plan to ask about their reimbursement rates for in-network and out-of-network mental health outpatient services. Also ask your insurance company about telehealth coverage!
